Banana Cake Recipe

ingredients

  • flour
  • tsp soda
  • salt
  • pd cinnamon
  • eggs
  • sugar
  • vegetable oil
  • vanilla essence
  • mashed ripe bananas
  • apples

procedure

  1. 3 cups (360 grams) flour sifted with 1 tsp soda, 1tsp salt, 1tsp pd cinnamon set aside
  2. in a bowl beat 3 eggs and gradually add 2 cups (400 grams) sugar, continue beating till sugar dissolves
  3. add
    • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
    • 2 tsp vanilla essence
    • 2 cups mashed ripe bananas
    • 1 cup apples
  4. stir well till mixed add sifted flour and 1 cup chopped nuts and raisins
  5. pour into greased and floured tin
  6. bake at 350 degrees for 1 hr
